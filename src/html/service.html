What we do?

The entry of Health Care Online Medical Portal into the state dominated healthcare sector in 1996, saw the private health care system take root in Sri Lanka. The launch of the hospital and the overwhelming response it received from the people demonstrated a long felt need for superior healthcare in a pleasant environment.

As Sri Lanka's first premier multi-specialty hospital, Health Care was set up to mirror reputed hospitals in the region which offered advanced medical technology and expert medical care, thus eliminating the need for people to travel out of Sri Lanka for specialized medical treatment.
